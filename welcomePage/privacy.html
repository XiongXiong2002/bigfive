Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We do not store your query inputs or selected options. Only your final test score is recorded for result processing.

Your IP address is temporarily collected to protect against DDoS attacks but is not persistently stored (stored for a maximum of 10 minutes).

2. Data Usage

Your test scores are used solely for generating personality test results.

If you choose the Standard Test, your scores will be shared with the Xinghuo Large Model in China for further score analysis.

If you do not want your data to be shared, you may opt for the Quick Test, which does not involve external processing.

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not permanently store personal data or identifiable information.
