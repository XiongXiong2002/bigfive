About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I am an independent developer working on this project as part of my final year at the University of Liverpool. I am passionate about software development and psychological testing.

My Goal

This project aims to provide a fast, anonymous, and accurate personality testing experience based on the Big Five model. The goal is to make personality testing accessible without requiring user registration or data persistence.

Why This Project?

  • Privacy-Oriented: No login required, and user data is not stored persistently.
  • Scientific Approach: Built using the well-established Big Five personality model.
  • User-Friendly: Offers both Standard and Quick test options for flexibility.
